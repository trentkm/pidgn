Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Pidgn ("we", "our", "us") is a family messaging app. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: Email address, display name, and password (managed by Firebase Authentication).
  • Messages: Text messages, photos, and voice memos you send to other households. Messages are stored in our database to deliver them to recipients.
  • Household information: Household name, member list, and connection relationships between households.
  • Device tokens: Push notification tokens to deliver notifications to your device.
  • Usage data: Basic server logs (IP addresses, request timestamps) for security and debugging.

How We Use Your Data

  • Deliver messages between connected households
  • Send push notifications for new messages and read receipts
  • Authenticate your identity and protect your account
  • Maintain and improve the service

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using Google Firebase (Firestore, Authentication, and Cloud Storage) hosted in the United States. Media files (photos and voice memos) are stored in Firebase Storage with access restricted to household members only. All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. We use Firebase (Google) as our infrastructure provider. We may disclose data if required by law.

Data Retention

Messages and media are retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

Your Rights

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent and delete your account

Children's Privacy

Pidgn is designed for families. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us data without consent, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us at privacy@pidgn.app.
